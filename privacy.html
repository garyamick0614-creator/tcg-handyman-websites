Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

TCG Solutions (“we,” “us”) is a website design and IT services business based in Indiana. This policy explains what we collect when you submit a website inquiry through this site, and what we do with it. It’s short on purpose.

What we collect

  • Information you give us in the quote form: your name, business name, phone, email, services you offer, and service area.
  • Basic technical info every web server gets: your IP address, browser type, and which pages you visit. We use this only to keep the site online and catch abuse.

How we use it

  • To contact you about your website project — that’s the whole point of the form.
  • To send you the quote, design previews, and any follow-up messages about the work you asked us about.
  • To keep our records of who we’ve worked with.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your information.
  • We don’t share it with marketing partners, ad networks, or data brokers.
  • We don’t add you to any unrelated mailing lists.

Who sees your form submission

The owner of TCG Solutions and any team member directly working on your quote. The form data passes through Netlify (our hosting provider) before reaching us. Netlify’s own privacy policy is at netlify.com/privacy.

Cookies

This site does not use marketing or tracking cookies. Your browser may store essential cookies for things like remembering you submitted the form. That’s it.

Your rights

Want us to delete your submission? Email hello@tcgsolutions.net with “Delete my info” in the subject. We’ll do it within 7 days and confirm.
