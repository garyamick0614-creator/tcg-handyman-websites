About Mike

The guy in the truck is the guy on the phone.

I started Mike’s Handyman in 2014 after a decade of remodel work for a bigger outfit that started cutting corners. I wanted to run a one-truck operation the old way — answer the phone myself, show up when I said I would, charge what I said I’d charge, and clean up before I left.

That’s still the deal. My son James helps on bigger jobs; everyone else you see on-site is a long-time partner I trust with my own house. We’re fully licensed and insured for Marion, Hamilton, Hancock, Hendricks, Johnson, and Boone counties.

FAQ

Straight answers.

How do I get a price?

Fill out the form below or call (317) 555-0184 with a quick description and a photo or two. For most jobs we can quote over the phone or text within 24 hours. Bigger projects, we’ll come out and walk it with you — that visit is free.

Do I pay a deposit?

For jobs under $1,500 we don’t take a deposit. Pay when the work is done. For larger projects we ask for 50% on start and 50% on completion, in writing, so you know exactly where the money goes.

What payment do you take?

Cash, check, Zelle, Venmo, or any card (3% surcharge on cards to cover the processor — we don’t mark it up).

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Indianapolis handyman license #HM-2019-04477, $2M general liability through Auto-Owners. Happy to send the certificate of insurance before the first visit.

How fast can you come out?

Most weeks we have a slot in the next 2–5 business days. Emergency leaks and aging-in-place work get bumped to the next available morning when we can.

What if I’m not happy with the work?

Tell me before I leave the driveway and I’ll fix it. After I leave, give us a call — we warranty our labor for 90 days on most jobs and a full year on remodels. We’ve never had to walk away from a job, and we’d rather come back than have a customer feel stuck.

Do you do new construction?

No — we’re a repair-and-improve outfit. If you need a contractor for a full addition or new build, we’ll point you at someone we trust.

What hours do you work?

Monday–Friday 7:30am to 5pm. Saturday mornings for emergencies or as scheduled. We don’t work Sundays unless something’s actively leaking.
