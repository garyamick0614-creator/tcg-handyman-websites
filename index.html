FAQ

Straight answers.

Do I own the site, or are you renting it to me?

You own it. We’ll hand over the domain, the code, and the hosting login. Cancel us tomorrow and the site is still yours.

What if I don’t have a logo or good photos?

We’ll make a clean wordmark logo at no extra charge on Standard and Premium. For photos, your phone is fine — we’ll edit and crop them. If you’ve got nothing yet, we’ll use clean service icons until you do.

Are there monthly fees?

The build is one-time. The only ongoing costs are your domain (~$12–$20/yr, paid to a registrar) and optional hosting/updates at $10/mo if you want us to handle changes for you. Otherwise hosting is included free on a TCG subdomain.

How fast can you launch?

Basic: 3 business days from the time we have your content. Standard: 5–7. Premium: 7–10.

Do you set up Google so people can find me?

Yes — Standard and Premium tiers include Google Business Profile setup and on-page local SEO so you show up in “near me” searches.

What if I need changes later?

Standard includes 30 days of free minor tweaks. Premium includes 90 days. After that, small changes are $25 each or take the $10/mo plan and updates are unlimited.
