Install FAQ

The questions homeowners actually ask.

Will it hold? My drywall is… old.

Anything over 20 lb goes into a stud. If we can’t hit a stud where you want it, we’ll either suggest moving the install 2″ left or use a heavy-duty blind anchor rated for 4x the load. We’ll never use a plastic drywall anchor on anything that matters.

Brick or concrete walls (chimney, basement)?

Yes — we use Tapcon screws into pre-drilled brick or concrete. Bring a photo so we can confirm we’ll have the right bits and anchors before we arrive.

Can you mount a TV over a fireplace?

If the surround above the fireplace is masonry and not too hot in active use, yes. We bring an infrared thermometer to confirm temps below 120°F during a typical fire. We’ll recommend against it if your hearth gets above 140°F under normal use.

Will you assemble furniture I already bought?

Yes — bring on the IKEA. Most flat-pack pieces take 30–90 minutes. We can typically assemble 3–5 pieces in a 2-hour block. Anchor straps to the wall included on anything tippable.

Smart lock install?

Yes — including Schlage Encode, Yale Assure, August Smart Lock, and Level Bolt. We pair to your home Wi-Fi and the app, set the first user code, and walk you through adding family before we leave.

Aging-in-place — what’s in scope?

Grab bars, raised toilet seats and comfort-height toilet swaps, lever-handle door hardware, threshold ramps, hand-held shower wands, and motion-sensor night lighting. We’ll do an in-home walk-through and prioritize by risk if you’re prepping for a return from the hospital.
