Electrical FAQ

The questions homeowners actually ask.

Do you go inside the breaker panel?

No — that’s licensed-electrician territory. We work everything from the switch or outlet outward. If you need panel work, we’ll get you a same-day estimate from our partner Tim at Marion Electric (Indianapolis Lic. #EC-2017-08821) and coordinate the visit.

Can you add a new outlet where there isn’t one?

If we’re tapping an existing nearby circuit and pulling Romex through accessible joist space (basement ceiling, attic), often yes. If it requires fishing through finished walls or a new breaker, that’s the licensed-electrician partner.

Will my smart switch work with my phone after you install it?

Yes — we pair it to your home Wi-Fi and the manufacturer’s app while we’re on-site and walk you through one routine before we leave. Compatible with Lutron Caseta, Leviton Decora Smart, Kasa, Wemo, and most Alexa/Google Home setups.

Hardwired smoke alarm replacement?

Yes — like-for-like replacement of 120V interconnected smoke or smoke/CO units. We test the interconnect on every alarm before we leave.

What if the old box behind my fixture is shot?

Common — old plastic boxes, missing grounds, or wrong-size boxes for the new fixture happen often. We carry retrofit boxes, fan-rated braces, and pigtail kits on the truck. If the job needs more than that, we’ll stop and quote it in writing.

Aluminum wiring concerns?

If your home was built between 1965 and 1973 there’s a chance of aluminum branch wiring. We’ll spot it when we open a box; for aluminum splicing we use AlumiConn or COPALUM connectors and follow CPSC guidance, or refer you to our licensed-electrician partner for a full evaluation.
